Peanut Butter Banana Cookie Protein Shake

This is a rich and creamy recipe for a high calorie protein shake.

This recipe is great for those training to gain mass. With all of the ingredients we get close to 1000 calories, 60 grams of protein and x grams of carbohydrates. This can be used as a meal substitute, but I would still recommend getting these macros from whole foods as well.

Ingredients

You will need:

  • A cup or shaker
  • 4 tablespoon Peanut Butter
  • 12-14 fl Oz Milk, Almond Milk, etc
  • 1 cup of whole oats
  • 1 whole Banana
  • 1 full serving of Ghost Chip's Ahoy whey protein

Steps

  1. Dice a banana
  2. Take your cup or shaker and add your shaker ball if equipped.
  3. Add 4 tablespoons of your favorite peanut butter
  4. Add the diced banana
  5. Add the milk or almond milk
  6. Add the whey protein powder
  7. Shake or stir your protein shake until peanut butter and whey protein are thoroughly mixed in

Note that this mix can be made in a blender as well
