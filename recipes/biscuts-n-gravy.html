Biscuits N' Gravy

This is a rich and creamy recipe for biscuits n gravy.

This recipe is great for any meal of the day. A good nutritious balance of protein, carbs, sodium, you name it.

Ingredients

You will need:

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt + Sugar
  • Butter
  • Buttermilk

Ingredients for Gravy

  • Breakfast Sausage
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Salt + Pepper

Steps for Biscuits

  1. Preheat the oven to 425F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or silicone baking mat. Do not grease your baking sheet
  2. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl
  3. Use a pastry blender or your fingertips to quickly cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles a coarse meal with slightly larger butter lumps
  4. Stir in the buttermilk using a rubber spatula or fork, until the mixture forms a soft, slightly sticky ball.
  5. Transfer the dough to lightly floured surface and quickly form into a rough square. Be careful not to over mix.
  6. Pat dough into 1-inch thick rectangle. Fold the dough letter-style into thirds, then lightly pat the dough back out into another 1-inch rectangle. Try to handle the dough as quickly and lightly as possible. Repeat the folding 2 more times; after the final fold, press or lightly roll the dough into a Â½-inch-thick rectangle.
  7. Cut the biscuits using a 2.5 inch rouind cutter, pushing straight down. Do not twist! LPat and roll scraps for more biscuits
  8. Place biscuits on the prepared baking sheet, brush with butter, and bake until the tops are light brown, 10 to 15 minutes.

Steps for Gravy

  1. Cook the sausage in a large skillet over medium-high heat until no longer pink. Crumble the sausage as it cooks into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Reduce the heat to medium and sprinkle half of the flour over the sausage, stirring to dissolve it. Add the rest of the flour and stir for about 1 minute, until all of the flour dissolved.
  3. Slowly pour in the milk, stirring constantly. Cook the gravy, stirring frequently until it begins to thicken, about 10-15 minutes.
  4. Season the gravy with black pepper and seasoned salt and continue to cook until it is very thick.
  5. Take the fluffy biscuits, split them in two, then spoon the heavenly sausage gravy on top to serve.
