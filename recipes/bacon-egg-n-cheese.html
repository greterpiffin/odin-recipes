Bacon Egg N Cheese Sandwhich

This is a protein rich bacon-egg-n-cheese sandwhich.

This recipe is great for those training to gain mass. With all of the ingredients we get close to 800 calories, 50 grams of protein and x grams of carbohydrates. Great for breakfast, but also works for any meal.

Ingredients

You will need:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Slices of Bacon
  • 1 half tablespoon of butter
  • 1 Non-stick Frying Pan
  • 1 Salt + Pepper
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 1 Slices of your favorite cheese
  • A toaster

Steps

  1. Heat your non-stick pan to a medium heat
  2. Once your pan is sufficiently hot, place your bacon strips on the pan. Be sure to flip the strips frequently to get an even crisp.
  3. Once cooked, set bacon aside on a paper towel to track grease. Remove some bacon grease from your pan.
  4. Crack 2 eggs into the pan, using residual bacon grease to help cook the eggs to OVER MEDIUM
  5. Start toast halfway through cooking the eggs
  6. Break bacon strips and cheese slice in half, setting 1 strip and 1 slice of cheese each on 1 egg.
  7. Once toast pops, butter both slices and remove your eggs from the heat.
  8. Set toast on a plate, and place eggs down 1 at a time, 1 on top of the other down onto a slice of toast.
  9. Take your sandwhich and press down firmly. This will squish the sandwhich and break the yolks, giving you a nice runny egg sandwhich
